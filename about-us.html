Information of the School


Our school is called San Remigio National High School (SRNHS), and it is located in Region VII, Cebu's 4th district, in the Municipality of San Remigio, Barangay Poblacion, Goopio Street. It is previously named as San Remigio Barangay High School. The School ID is 303087, the School Type is Mother School, the Sub-Classification is DepED Managed, and the Class Organization is Monograde, according to our school information. Junior High School and Senior High School curriculums are used in our classes. If you want to contact us, you can do it through our Telephone Number - 435-9300 or through our Fax Number - 435-9300.

Mission


To protect and promote the right of every Filipino to quality, equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education where:
  • Students learn in a child-friendly, gender-sensitive, safe, and motivating environment
  • Teachers facilitate learning and constantly nurture every learner
  • Administrators and staff, as stewards of the institution, ensure an enabling and supportive environment for effective learning to happen
  • Family, community, and other stakeholders are actively engaged and share responsibility for developing life-long learners
